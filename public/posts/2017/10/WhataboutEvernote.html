What about Evernote?

Friday, 3 November 2017

Yes, I abandoned Evernote years ago. But what if? :)

I was re-reading my post about Digital Recordkeeping and it prompted me to reconsider using Evernote. Evernote was my "junk drawer" but I switched to putting everything into DEVONthink instead. This has caused my DEVONthink databases to become messy. I don't like DEVONthink to be messy, that's what Evernote was for.

So, I've installed Evernote again to see if it's still feasible as a junk drawer and if it helps keep DEVONthink clean.
