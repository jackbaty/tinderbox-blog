What if no one reads this?

Sunday, 12 November 2017

I ask myself, "What if no one reads what I write here?" The answer is that it doesn't matter. I share my writing publically on the off chance that someone might bump into it and find it useful. More importantly, I write so that I can revisit things later. Sometimes years later.

I think we are well advised to keep on nodding terms with the people we used to be, whether we find them attractive company or not. -- Joan Didion
